🔹 STEP 5: CONFIGURE SECRETS (3 minutes)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

→ Open: https://huggingface.co/spaces/izhan5/whatsapp-toolkit/settings

Scroll to "Repository secrets"

Add these 3 secrets (click "New secret" for each):

Secret 1:
  Name: GROQ_API_KEY
  Value: [paste your Groq API key]
  → Click "Add secret"

Secret 2:
  Name: SUPABASE_URL
  Value: [paste your Supabase URL]
  → Click "Add secret"

Secret 3:
  Name: SUPABASE_ANON_KEY
  Value: [paste your Supabase anon key]
  → Click "Add secret"

After adding all 3:
→ Click "Factory reboot" button at bottom of settings page
